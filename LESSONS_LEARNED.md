## Technical Issues

- EKS quest failed during the event  -  root cause to be investigated
- Impacted team scores significantly; balance may have been very different if quest worked
- Especially affected new attendees who were stuck on the right path

## Stream Format Feedback

- Stream was largely one-directional during the 2h gameplay window (intentional for first edition  -  teams needed to focus)
- Feedback received: lack of real-time commentary and score drama reduced competitive tension
- "A few boxes of random views" did not convey the feeling of global scale
- Suggestion: regular check-ins (every 30 min), score commentary, leaderboard moments
- Suggestion: live location check-ins with UG leaders (with consent) to show global scale
- Suggestion: Gamemasters popping in for blitz interviews with locations

## Ideas for Next Edition

- [ ] Leaderboard integration: show real-time scores during gameplay (password-protected API  -  contribution needed)
